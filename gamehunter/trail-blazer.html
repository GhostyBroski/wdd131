How We Started Hunting:

When we started out, there wasn't exactly a "game plan" set out in front of us. I mean, we just wanted to be able to spend our time enjoying well crafted games, sharing laughs, and making memories along the way. So, to commemorate and show off our journey, we created "Trailblazer"! This nifty game will give you some good insights to how we felt exploring for answers and ways to bring everything together, with it all possibly being able to change at the drop of a hat. We hope that you enjoy your journey, whatever that may look like, just as much as us.
